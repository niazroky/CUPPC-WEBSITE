About CUPPC

CUPPC stands for Chittagong University Physics Programming Club. As the name suggests, our goal is to integrate computer programming with the study of physics.

In today's world, simply reading physics books isn't enough to become a great physicist. Programming plays a crucial role in accessing and utilizing various technological resources that can enhance your understanding of physics. We have observed a significant gap between technology and physics in our department. To bridge this gap, we established CUPPC.

Our club aims to:

  • Assist teachers with their research endeavors by leveraging programming skills.
  • Engage students in meaningful research projects that blend physics and programming.
  • Organize workshops, seminars, and collaborative projects to foster innovation and academic growth.

Join us at CUPPC as we bridge the gap between technology and physics, empowering students to contribute to research and advance their understanding through practical application.
